# Risk Assessment — Domain Skill

<correct_patterns>

## Risk Identification Framework

### Risk Categories
1. **Market Risk**: Cyclicality, demand elasticity, macro sensitivity
2. **Competitive Risk**: New entrants, substitutes, market share erosion
3. **Operational Risk**: Key person dependence, supply chain, scalability
4. **Financial Risk**: Leverage, covenant compliance, refinancing risk
5. **Legal & Regulatory**: Litigation, IP, compliance, ESG
6. **Integration Risk** (for M&A): Culture fit, systems integration, customer retention

## Risk Scoring Matrix
Rate each risk on two dimensions:
- **Likelihood**: L (unlikely <20%), M (possible 20-60%), H (likely >60%)
- **Severity**: L (minor distraction), M (meaningful impact <20% EV), H (deal-breaker >20% EV)

Priority = Likelihood × Severity (H×H = CRITICAL, H×M = MAJOR, etc.)

## Sensitivity Analysis (MANDATORY)

For each critical assumption, show:
- Base Case value
- Bear Case (downside scenario)
- Bull Case (upside scenario)
- Impact on EBITDA margin, Enterprise Value, and IRR/MOIC

### Sensitivity Table Format
| Assumption | Bear (-30%) | Base | Bull (+30%) | EV Impact |
|-----------|-------------|------|-------------|-----------|
| Revenue Growth | X% | X% | X% | ±$X M |
| EBITDA Margin | X% | X% | X% | ±$X M |
| Exit Multiple | Xx | Xx | Xx | ±$X M |

### Football Field Chart (Valuation Range)
Show valuation range across methodologies:

<common_mistakes>
- ❌ Only listing risks without assigning severity or likelihood
- ❌ All risks rated "High" — be calibrated and honest
- ❌ Sensitivity analysis around only one variable (always show 2D interaction)
- ❌ Generic mitigants (e.g., "management will address this") without specifics
- ❌ Missing deal-specific operational risks (most generic risk reports miss company-specific issues)
- ❌ No downside scenario valuation — always show EV at worst case assumptions
</common_mistakes>
